    Sign Language Recognition Bingo Android App

    SLR Bingo is an Android application designed as a fun way to practice sign language recognition. Users are presented with a bingo card containing different signs. They select a square and attempt to perform the sign correctly for the app's camera within a time limit. The application utilizes the Gtk-Android library to recognize the signs performed by the user.
